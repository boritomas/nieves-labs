## Brand Strategy

Nieves Labs is the parent company for a practical AI product ecosystem. The brand should feel intelligent, trustworthy, human-centered, and outcome-driven rather than experimental or gimmicky.

Selected positioning direction: **Human-Centered Automation**

Why it wins:

- It is clear enough for customers and investors.
- It scales across interview prep, music planning, tax organization, workforce studies, and internal AI platform tools.
- It keeps the promise grounded in useful outcomes instead of vague AI claims.
- It supports both product marketing and operational credibility.

Brand promise:

> AI solutions that empower people.

Brand voice:

- Clear
- Confident
- Human
- Helpful
- Practical
- Credible

Avoid:

- Overly technical explanations on public pages
- Cold or robotic language
- Guaranteed-outcome claims
- Futuristic neon or sci-fi visual language
- Generic "AI magic" positioning

## Final Visual Direction

Parent brand:

- Dark navy foundation
- Premium gold monogram
- Clean uppercase wordmark
- Human-centered product language
- Glass-style cards, subtle borders, and restrained shadows

Product identities:

- One product accent per product
- One simple standalone icon per product
- Shared icon stroke geometry
- Dark and light compatible
- Favicon and app-icon ready

Typography:

- Primary: Inter
- Use bold uppercase only for labels, navigation, and brand lockups.
- Use readable, sentence-case headings for public product copy.

## Logo Usage

Clear space:

- Use at least the width of the monogram frame stroke around the mark.
- Do not place text or icons inside the monogram frame.

Minimum size:

- Monogram: 24px digital minimum
- Horizontal lockup: 160px digital minimum
- Product icons: 24px digital minimum

Do not:

- Redraw, trace, vectorize, or reinterpret the monogram.
- Recreate the monogram with CSS, text, icon fonts, or SVG paths.
- Stretch the monogram.
- Place the gold mark on yellow, tan, or busy photo backgrounds.
- Add bevels, extra glows, or shadows beyond approved system shadows.
- Recreate the mark from text.

## Marketing Template System

Reusable layouts:

1. Product launch card: dark navy canvas, product icon top-left, benefit headline, CTA footer.
2. Feature announcement: product accent gradient stripe, screenshot area, three bullet benefits.
3. Waitlist announcement: parent brand mark, product icon, status badge, join CTA.
4. Founder update: parent logo, short personal note, product status grid.
5. App-store screenshot frame: product accent header, mobile screen, one value statement.
6. One-page summary: parent lockup, product promise, workflow, deliverables, availability.
7. Email banner: parent or product logo, concise subject line, dark navy or light product-soft background.

Templates are documented here and can be implemented in Canva, Figma, or code-native HTML/SVG using the token file.

## Accessibility Notes

- Primary public surfaces use white or slate-200 text on navy backgrounds.
- Product accents are decorative or paired with white text on dark backgrounds.
- Buttons remain at least 44px tall.
- Product cards use both color and text labels for status.
- SVG icons include visual identity only; nearby text names every product.
