# Nieves Labs Product Catalog

**Last Updated**: July 2026

## Public Messaging Rules

- Describe products by the customer problem they solve.
- Use neutral public-facing language.
- Focus on customer benefits and outcomes.
- Reference industry-standard tools only.
- Do not reference specific employers, private client workflows, or internal operational details.

## Products

### AnswerBrief AI

Role-specific interview preparation that turns a resume and job posting into an interview brief, story prompts, and prep checklist.

### Tax Buddy

Tax document organization support that helps customers collect forms, receipts, notes, and missing-item summaries before professional review.

### Tax Appeal Buddy

Property tax appeal preparation support with evidence organization, comparable-property worksheets, appeal packet structure, and hearing prep notes.

### Interview Coach

Interview practice planning with a coaching plan, question bank, response improvement checklist, and mock interview script.

### Workforce Study

Operational workforce/process study package that creates a concise report, workflow map, opportunity backlog, and executive summary.

### MixPilot AI

AI-powered DJ set planning for parties, events, workouts, lounges, and creators. MixPilot AI turns playlists into structured set plans with energy flow, transition ideas, cue notes, and event-ready sequencing.

### Nieves AI Platform

AI product and automation planning package for teams that need architecture, workflow design, integration planning, and implementation roadmap support.

## Operational Model

Every product follows the same production workflow:

1. Package selection
2. Stripe Checkout or free beta start
3. Webhook validation
4. Order creation
5. Secure intake and uploads
6. Workflow execution
7. Drive/Gmail/App Script automation
8. Deliverable generation
9. Admin visibility
